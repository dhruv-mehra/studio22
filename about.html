Studio 22 Productions

Studio 22 is a student-run production company that exists to provide extracurricular filmmaking opportunities not offered in production classes. We fund seven to eight student films that are showcased at the end of each school year. We aim to produce ambitious, high-quality student films and serve as an active agent in the development of Studio 22 projects.

Multi Cultural Filmmaker's Collective

Multicultural Filmmakers Collective (MultiCulti) is dedicated to supporting and uplifting the voices of marginalized communities through film. As an organization, Multicultural Filmmakers Collective purpose is to nurture and promote multicultural student filmmakers at Northwestern University as well as supporting student films that center on multicultural experiences and concerns. In order to better serve and share narratives concerning marginalized communities, MultiCulti defines multicultural as including, but not limited to, underrepresented races, ethnicities, religions, genders, sexual orientations and socioeconomic statuses.
