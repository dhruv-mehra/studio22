Finding Humanity

a Studio 22 Film

Written and Directed by Sumin You

Produced by Scarlet Li and Claire Stevens

Starring: Jiwon Sung, Griffin Johnston, Yuna Sung

An alien with hostile intent finds himself stranded in a small town in Korea and meets a welcoming family, finding humanity in an unexpected place.

Meet the Filmmakers

Scarlet Li

Co-Producer

RTVF and Political Science student from Beijing.

Sumin You

Writer/Director

When I was younger, I used to be scared of aliens. Now, I see one in the mirror. When I was writing Finding Humanity, I feel like I was in a stage of frustration. I was frustrated that after I came to the United States, things that were once so natural to me, things that laid rock hard foundation to who I was as a person, were no longer natural. I liked NU, my new environment, but I missed the feeling of "being at home". Now that I think of it, that may be the reason why Finding Humanity ends the way it does. (No spoilers!) Now I don't feel that way, but I feel like this film will always be a bookmark for me to remember that feeling, and to remember that better things are coming, like the production of this movie for me.
Finding Humanity would have remained only as a wild imagination of mine if it weren't for Scarlet and Claire for being wonderful filmmakers and helping me throughout every detail of the journey. I thank you all, Jiwon, Griffin, Jerry, Maggie, Sue, Thomas, Camille, Peter, Marisa, Shannon, Mega, Sukwon, Hannah, Liz... the list goes on. Thank you Yuna, our beloved Yeboon, for not only being on board with a crazy idea from a complete stranger, but also for being a true inspiration, a strong Korean immigrant woman that I can look up to. Thank you San for walking with me the moment I thought of Finding Humanity.
I'm excited to see this film come to life in our near future and until then, hang on tight!

Claire Stevens

Co-Producer

RTVF and Creative Writing third culture student from Manila.
