Playland

a Bindley film

Written and Directed by Gregory Voelkel

Produced by Jenna Lindsay Trattner and Loie Gilbert

Starring: Madeline Gross, Charles Andrew Gardner, Alec Citron, Jack Goss

Sophie, a mother grieving the loss of her son, turns to extraordinary and supernatural means in an attempt to communicate with her child.

Meet the Filmmakers

Jenna Lindsay Trattner

Co-Producer

This project has meant a great deal to me mainly because of the people who made it happen. Whether you were attached from day one, joined in its final stages, passed through in any way -- thank you. I feel very honored to have crossed paths with each and every one of you, this whirlwind of a manic state brought some of the most wonderful memories. I was truly just happy to be there with y'all.

Gregory Voelkel

Writer/Director

My name is Gregory Voelkel and welcome to our film, Playland. In what has been an extremely trying year so far, I am ever grateful for the hard work of all of those involved in this production. It was, without a doubt, one of the most rewarding experiences I've ever had, and I'm thankful for the opportunity to have worked with so many wonderful, talented, supportive and driven people. None of this could have ever happened without this cast and crew.
While we hoped to be able to share this film in-person, one of the happy accidents of it being a virtual premiere is that I (and all of you) can watch and enjoy this film and all of the other wonderful films with their families and friends. And family is, at its core, Playland is about: its characters were inspired by my family heroes, whose love and sacrifice is always in service of others. And truthfully, I like to think of this cast and crew as one big family. I hope you enjoy!

Loie Gilbert

Co-Producer

Working on Playland was one of my best experiences at Northwestern. I want to thank the cast and crew for not only working so hard, but keeping a positive energy throughout the shoot. I know when this is all over we'll find a way to get together and celebrate all our hard work!
